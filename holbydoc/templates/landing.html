Key Features

Course Management

Create, organize and share structured educational content, such as courses, modules or learning paths, to facilitate training, understanding and access to knowledge in a logical and efficient way.

Rich Content

Whether you're writing technical documentation, tutorials, or blog posts, the editor provides all the tools you need to produce clear, structured, and visually appealing content.

Collaboration

Easily share knowledge, tips, and resources with peers in the Holberton community. Whether it's code, articles, or helpful links, our platform makes collaboration smooth and impactful.

About HolbyDoc

HolbyDoc was born from a personal experience as a Holberton School student. After spending countless hours searching for scattered resources and documentation, I realized the need for a centralized, well-organized platform that would make learning more efficient for all students.

This project is part of my Portfolio Project at Holberton School, but it's much more than just an assignment. It's a solution to a real problem faced by students every day.
